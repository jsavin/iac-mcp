# The Complete Vision: Frontier + AI Bridge

## The Two Projects

### Project 1: Frontier Modernization (Separate Effort)
**What:** Updating the nearly 40-year-old Frontier (GPL open source) codebase
**Goals:**
- 64-bit compatibility
- True thread-safety
- Multi-user capabilities
- Modern macOS (and eventually cross-platform)

**Status:** Active development, will take time

**What it provides:**
- Object database (central storage)
- UserTalk scripting environment
- Outliner-based development
- Local workflow environment
- Multi-user collaboration
- The **foundation layer** for local computing

### Project 2: IAC MCP Bridge (This Project)
**What:** Bridging LLM capabilities to locally installed applications
**Goals:**
- Just-In-Time Discovery of native app capabilities
- Universal Inter-Application Communication layer
- AI-powered workflow orchestration
- Connect online AI activity with local apps

**Status:** Planning/early development

**What it provides:**
- Dynamic discovery of any installed app
- AI understanding of app capabilities
- Execution layer (AppleEvents, COM, D-Bus)
- The **bridge layer** between AI and native apps
